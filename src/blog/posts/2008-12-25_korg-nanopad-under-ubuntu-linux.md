# Korg NanoPad under Ubuntu Linux

I just got a [Korg NanoPad](http://www.korg.com/gear/info.asp?A_PROD_NO=nanoSeries) for Christmas.  I wasn’t sure how well it was going to work under Linux, but I’m happy to report that snd-usb-audio recognized it right away when I plugged it in.  Even the [Kontrol Editor](http://www.korg.com/service/downloadinfo.asp?DID=1458) software (Windows or Mac only) worked under WINE.
